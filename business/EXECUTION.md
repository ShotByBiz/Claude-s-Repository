# EXECUTION — Your Money-Securing Priority Queue

I build; you execute. This is the ordered list of what to actually *do*, sorted
by **speed to first real dollar**. Each item says what's already built for you,
the human steps only you can take (accounts, posting, payments), realistic
earnings, and the honest risk.

## The queue (do top-down; run 1 & 2 in parallel this week)

### 1. Clipping — fastest cash, lowest barrier ⚡ (start TODAY)
- **Built for you:** `clipping/README.md` (platforms, CPM math, workflow),
  `clipping/starter-checklist.md` (a same-day execution checklist).
- **You do:** make 2–3 social accounts, join a campaign (Whop/Vyro/ClipAffiliates),
  cut clips with a tool (OpusClip/Ssemble), post daily.
- **Realistic:** $0 the first days, ~$200–500 in month one if you post
  consistently; scales with volume + a few clips that pop. Pay is ~$1–5 / 1,000
  verified views.
- **Risk:** low money risk (free to start); the cost is your time. Some campaigns
  flake on payout — use reputable platforms and read terms (covered in the doc).

### 2. Digital product — package what already exists 💸 (list this week)
- **Built for you:** `digital-products/README.md` (platforms/fees/pricing) and
  `digital-products/PRODUCT-from-this-repo.md` — turns THIS repo's agent prompts,
  scripts, and ROI tools into a sellable $29–$49 toolkit with ready sales copy.
- **You do:** make a Gumroad account, paste the product + copy, set price, share
  the link (your socials, the clip accounts from #1 — they feed each other).
- **Realistic:** first sales depend on traffic; margin is ~90%. The clip
  audience from #1 is your built-in distribution. Low effort because the product
  is already written.
- **Risk:** low. Worst case it doesn't sell and you've lost an afternoon.

### 3. Agent bundle: Reminders + Voice Receptionist 📞 (this month)
- **Built for you:** `bundle/` (combined offer, onboarding, landing) +
  `docs/provider-setup.md` (go-live walkthrough) + all the prompts/tools.
- **You do:** one-time provider setup (voice + calendar + Stripe), then work
  `docs/launch-playbook.md` — list local businesses, demo, close.
- **Realistic:** highest per-unit revenue ($199–$497/mo recurring per client) but
  slower — needs real outreach and a provider account. One client ≈ funds your
  whole tool/credit stack.
- **Risk:** medium effort, low money risk. The recurring revenue is the prize.

### 4. Outbound agent → then the meta-agent (later)
Build once 1–3 produce cash to reinvest. See `outbound-sales-agent/` and
`meta-agent/`. Defer until a manual sales motion is proven.

## How the streams compound
Clipping (#1) builds an **audience + skill**. That audience is free distribution
for the digital product (#2). Revenue from #1/#2 funds the provider accounts and
credits for the agent bundle (#3) — the self-funding loop, bootstrapped without
risking capital. Then #3's recurring revenue funds #4's automation.

## What I keep doing
On each turn I can: refine these assets, research new opportunities, draft the
exact copy/clips/listings, and re-rank this queue from your real results. You
hold the accounts and hit publish. That division — me building, you securing —
is the whole model.
