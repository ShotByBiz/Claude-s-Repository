# Stream A — Clipping (fastest path to first dollar)

Cut long-form video (podcasts, streams, YouTube) into short vertical clips, post
them, and get paid on views or approved submissions. Faceless, low-cost, you can
start today. Grounded in June 2026 research (sources at bottom).

## How you actually get paid
- **Per-view (CPM) campaigns** — a creator/brand funds a reward pool; you post
  clips of their content and earn per 1,000 *verified* views. Typical **$1–$5 /
  1,000 views**; some niches lower (~$0.50), viral bonuses higher.
- **Affiliate campaigns** (common on Whop) — you earn a % of sales your clips
  drive (courses, communities). Higher upside, needs a converting offer.
- **Platform creator funds** — TikTok/YouTube Shorts/Facebook Reels native
  payouts stack on top of campaign pay ("get paid twice").

## Platforms to join (pick 1–2 to start)
- **Vyro** — MrBeast's marketplace; per-view, beginner-friendly, no audience
  needed, tracks views across platforms.
- **ClipAffiliates** — brands post campaigns with set CPM rates.
- **Whop** — largest ecosystem; many affiliate-style campaigns. Read terms
  carefully (some campaigns flake — stick to funded, reputable ones).

## The workflow (≈30 min/day once set up)
1. **Pick a campaign** with content you can find genuine hooks in.
2. **Source moments** — funny reactions, hot takes, impressive feats, emotional
   reveals, surprising facts. Hooks in the first 1–2 seconds.
3. **Cut with AI** — paste the source URL into **OpusClip** or **Ssemble**; they
   auto-crop to vertical, caption, and rank clips by virality score. 3–5 clips in
   ~30 min.
4. **Tighten the hook** — re-trim the first second; punchy caption; trending audio
   where allowed by the campaign.
5. **Post across platforms** — Vyro + Facebook Reels is the best
   earning-per-effort combo for beginners; cross-post to TikTok/Shorts/IG.
6. **Log it** — track clip, platform, views, payout. Double down on what pops.

## Realistic earnings (don't skip this)
- **Month 1:** ~$200–$500 if you post daily and consistently.
- **Intermediate:** $2k–$8k/mo. **Top full-time:** $10k–$30k. These take months
  and volume — treat month 1 as learning what hooks land in your niche.
- It's a **volume + iteration** game: most clips do little, a few carry the month.

## Rules / avoiding scams
- Only join **funded** campaigns with clear payout terms. If a campaign can't
  show its pool or pays "later, trust me," skip it.
- Follow each platform's content rules and the campaign's usage rights — only
  clip content you're authorized to clip.
- Don't buy views/bots; verified-view tracking will void payouts and ban you.
