# Day-One Clipping Kit — Niche Pick + 10 Copy-Paste Hooks

So you can start today without deciding anything from scratch. Pick the niche,
grab a campaign in that lane, and use these hooks as the on-screen text / first
line of each clip.

## Your niche pick: **Podcast / Interview clips (business & "hot takes")**

Why this one for a beginner:
- **Endless source content** — thousands of long podcasts to clip from.
- **Hooks are easy to spot** — a strong opinion, a surprising story, a debate.
- **Travels across platforms** — the same clip works on TikTok, Reels, Shorts,
  Facebook.
- **High campaign demand** — creators in this space fund clipping pools heavily.
- **Faceless by default** — it's the guest's face, never yours.

> Backup niche if podcasts bore you: **Gaming highlights/fails** (big reactions,
> clear hooks). Pick the one you'd actually watch for hours.

## The day-one play
1. Join a per-view campaign in the podcast/business lane (Vyro or ClipAffiliates).
2. Open one of their source episodes; scrub for a 15–40s moment with tension or a
   surprising line.
3. Auto-cut in OpusClip/Ssemble; keep the 3 best.
4. Slap one hook below on as the on-screen title; captions on; payoff at the end.
5. Post to TikTok + Reels + Shorts + Facebook. Log views.

## 10 ready hooks (with how to use each)

1. **"He said the quiet part out loud…"** — over a guest's blunt/controversial line.
2. **"This is why you're still broke."** — over money/mindset advice. Curiosity + sting.
3. **"Nobody talks about THIS part of success."** — over a counterintuitive take.
4. **"Wait for his face at the end."** — over a reaction/reveal moment. Drives watch-time.
5. **"She just ended the whole debate in 20 seconds."** — over a sharp rebuttal.
6. **"I replayed this 5 times."** — over an impressive or mind-bending point.
7. **"The advice that changed how I work."** — over a practical, tactical tip.
8. **"99% of people get this completely wrong."** — over a myth being busted.
9. **"He almost walked off after this question."** — over a tense exchange.
10. **"Save this before it gets taken down."** — over a spicy/bold claim. Drives saves.

## Hook rules (so they actually work)
- The hook must match the clip — bait that doesn't pay off kills your retention.
- First 1–2 seconds only. If the moment builds slowly, start *after* the build.
- Keep on-screen text to 3–7 words, big and readable.
- End on the punchline or an open loop so people rewatch.

## Track these 4 things per clip
`hook used | platform | 24h views | 7d views`
After ~20 clips you'll see which 2–3 hooks are *your* winners. Make more of those.
