# Model 3 — Content Marketing + Lead Gen Agency-in-a-Box

Agents that research a niche, produce SEO-aware blog/social content, and capture
+ nurture leads. Sold as a monthly retainer to small businesses.

## Honest scope
Here: the content engine prompt, an SEO brief template, and an editorial-calendar
generator. Publishing needs the client's CMS/social access (their accounts) and
ideally a human review pass — AI-generated content that's published unread is how
agencies lose clients to thin/inaccurate posts.

## Contents
- `content-engine-prompt.md` — research → outline → draft agent
- `seo-brief-template.md` — the brief every piece is built from
- `editorial_calendar.py` — generates a dated, themed content calendar

## Economics
- Retainer **$500–$2,000/mo** for N pieces + distribution + a lead magnet.
- Margin is high once templatized, but **quality control is the cost** — budget
  human/expert review time; it's what separates a retainer from churn.

## Why this is the riskiest of the five
Commoditized (everyone has an AI writer), slow to show ROI (SEO takes months),
and trust-sensitive (one wrong published claim burns the client). Win only with
a **narrow niche** + **real subject-matter review** + a measurable lead magnet.
Treat content as the lead-gen vehicle, not the product — the product is leads.
