# SEO Brief Template

One brief per piece. The engine builds nothing without it.

```
Target query:          e.g. "how much does a tankless water heater cost"
Search intent:         informational | commercial | transactional
Primary audience:      who is searching and what they fear/want
Funnel stage:          top (educate) | mid (compare) | bottom (decide)
Angle / unique take:    the reason THIS piece beats page 1 today
Must-cover questions:   the real sub-questions the searcher has
Conversion goal:        book call | download lead magnet | request quote
Lead magnet to offer:   the gated thing that captures the email
Internal links:         pages to link to
Word count target:      match intent, not a vanity number
Claims needing review:   (filled by engine, verified by human)
```

## Lead magnet is mandatory
Content without a capture mechanism is a cost center. Every piece points to a
gated asset (checklist, calculator, quote, guide) that turns a reader into a
known lead. Track: visits → magnet conversions → leads → booked → won. The
client pays for the bottom of that funnel, not the word count at the top.
