# Stream B — Digital Products

High-margin (80–95%), make-once-sell-forever assets. Grounded in June 2026
research (sources at bottom). The smartest first product for *you* already exists
in this repo — see `PRODUCT-from-this-repo.md`.

## What sells (beginner-friendly, fast to make)
- **Templates / printables** — social calendars, planners, niche spreadsheets
  ($9–$29). Reusable layouts; Canva-makeable.
- **AI prompt packs** — buyers want faster results from tools they already pay
  for. One of the newest high-margin categories ($15–$49).
- **E-books / mini-guides** — simple to make, no inventory, great margins.
- **Notion / productivity systems** — CRMs, trackers, dashboards ($19–$79).

## Where to sell (and the fees)
| Platform | Fee | Best for |
|----------|-----|----------|
| **Gumroad** | 10% + $0.50 (30% via Discover) | Pointing your own audience at a checkout |
| **Etsy** | ~6.5% + $0.20/listing | Built-in search traffic / discovery |
| **Dodo Payments** | 4% + $0.40, merchant-of-record | Global sales tax handled for you |

Common play: **Etsy for discovery, Gumroad for your audience/bundles.** Your
clipping accounts (Stream A) are free traffic to a Gumroad link.

## Pricing ladder (proven structure)
- **Starter $19** — small sample (20–30 prompts / a single template).
- **Pro $49–$97** — full library + a short how-to video.
- **Agency license $197+** — same assets + commercial/multi-client license.

Anchor on the middle tier; let buyers self-select.

## Why this pairs with everything else
- The product can literally be the agent prompts/toolkits in this repo.
- Clipping drives traffic; the product monetizes that traffic without you
  trading more hours.
- Each sale funds API credits/tools for the agent businesses.

## Execution (you, ~an afternoon)
1. Pick the product (start with `PRODUCT-from-this-repo.md`).
2. Make a Gumroad account; create the product; paste the provided sales copy.
3. Set price ($29–$49), upload the files (zip the relevant `business/` folders).
4. Publish; drop the link in your clip-account bios and any socials.
5. Iterate price/title from what converts.
