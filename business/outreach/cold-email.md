# Cold Email Sequence (5 touches)

Rules that matter more than the copy:
- **One clear ask** per email (a 10-min call or a "yes, send info").
- **Specific to them** — reference their business by name; mention you called and
  what happened (it's true and it's the whole point).
- **Short.** Under 90 words. Mobile-readable.
- **No attachments, one link max.** Avoids spam filters.
- Comply with CAN-SPAM: real name, physical address in footer, working
  unsubscribe. For SMS/calls, comply with TCPA (consent, hours, do-not-call).

---

## Touch 1 — Day 0 (the proof-of-problem opener)

> **Subject:** called {{BUSINESS_NAME}} today
>
> Hi {{FIRST_NAME}},
>
> I called {{BUSINESS_NAME}} this morning around {{TIME}} and it went to
> voicemail. No worries — but that's the reason I'm writing.
>
> I set up AI receptionists for {{VERTICAL}} offices that answer every call
> 24/7, book appointments, and text you the details. Most clients were missing
> 5–10 calls a day without realizing it.
>
> Worth a 10-minute look? I can have one answering your line as a demo by
> tomorrow.
>
> — {{YOUR_NAME}}

## Touch 2 — Day 2 (the number)

> **Subject:** ~${{LOST_PER_MONTH}}/mo (rough math)
>
> {{FIRST_NAME}} — quick napkin math for {{BUSINESS_NAME}}: if you miss ~6 calls
> a day and even 1 in 3 would've booked, that's around **${{LOST_PER_MONTH}}/mo**
> walking out the door. Our service is $297/mo and answers all of them.
>
> Want me to spin up a live demo on your line? Reply "demo."

## Touch 3 — Day 5 (the demo offer / let it sell itself)

> **Subject:** hear it for yourself
>
> Easiest way to get this: I'll point a demo receptionist at a test number and
> you call it like a customer would. Takes 2 minutes and it'll book you a fake
> appointment on the spot. Want the number?

## Touch 4 — Day 9 (social proof + risk reversal)

> **Subject:** answer your calls or it's free
>
> {{FIRST_NAME}}, last note for now. {{VERTICAL}} offices we work with stopped
> losing after-hours and lunch-rush calls within a week. There's no contract and
> a 14-day "it answers your calls or you don't pay" guarantee. 10 minutes?

## Touch 5 — Day 14 (the breakup)

> **Subject:** should I close your file?
>
> Haven't heard back so I'll assume the timing's off and stop here. If missed
> calls ever become a priority, just reply and I'll set up a demo same day.
> Thanks {{FIRST_NAME}}.

## Where to find leads (ethically, public data)
Google Maps / Google Business Profiles, Yelp, vertical directories. Filter for
businesses whose listed phone goes to voicemail when you call during the day —
that's a pre-qualified prospect who is *actively losing money right now*.
