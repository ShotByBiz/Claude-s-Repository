# Pricing & Unit Economics

## Packaging (anchor on the middle tier)

| Tier | Price/mo | Setup | Includes |
|------|----------|-------|----------|
| **Starter** | $197 | $0 | Inbound answering, FAQ, message capture, daily email summary. 1 number, business hours. |
| **Pro** ⭐ | $297 | $0 | Everything in Starter + appointment booking into their calendar, after-hours + emergency tagging, 24/7, weekly performance report. |
| **Premium** | $497 | $250 | Everything in Pro + SMS follow-up, reminder/no-show reduction, 2 numbers, CRM sync, monthly optimization call. |

Most local service businesses land on **Pro**. Lead with Pro; let price-sensitive
prospects self-select down to Starter rather than offering it first.

## Why these prices work

Your cost floor per client (voice minutes + LLM tokens) typically runs
**$15–$60/mo** depending on call volume. At $297 that's an **80–95% gross
margin**. The price is justified entirely by the ROI calculator output — if a
prospect loses $3–5k/mo to missed calls, $297 is a rounding error to them.

## Cost components to track per client

- Voice provider per-minute (Retell/Vapi/Bland): ~$0.07–$0.15/min
- LLM tokens (the agent's reasoning): a few cents per call
- Phone number rental: ~$1–3/mo
- Your time: front-loaded at setup, near-zero after

## Targets

- **Gross margin:** keep above 80%. If a heavy-volume client drops below, move
  them to Premium or a usage tier.
- **CAC:** with cold outreach + live demos, target < 1 month of revenue
  recovered (i.e., pay back acquisition in the first 30 days).
- **Churn:** the killer. The retention lever is the **weekly report** showing
  calls answered + bookings made — it makes the value visible so they don't
  cancel. Never let a client forget what you save them.

## Contract & billing

- Month-to-month, no long contract (lowers the close barrier). The value report
  is what retains, not a lock-in clause.
- Bill via Stripe (payment link or subscription). 14-day "answer your calls or
  it's free" guarantee removes risk and closes faster.
- Setup fee waived on annual prepay (improves cash flow and cuts churn).
