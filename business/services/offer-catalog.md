# Productized Offer Catalog

Fixed scope, fixed price, fixed turnaround = minimal back-and-forth and an easy
"here's your finished product" handoff. Prices are market-aware starting points;
raise them as your reviews grow.

## Packaging rules
- Always state **what's included AND what's not** (kills scope creep).
- Offer 3 tiers per gig (basic/standard/premium) — most buyers pick the middle.
- One round of revisions included; extra rounds are a paid add-on.
- Bulk/recurring options turn one-off buyers into monthly revenue.

## Margin note
These are near-pure-margin (your time + tokens). The constraint is throughput and
quality, not cost — so price for *value and turnaround*, and raise prices as the
review count climbs.
