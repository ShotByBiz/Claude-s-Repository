# Sales Automation — Listings, Templates, Intake, Delivery

The goal: automate ~80% of communication so you mostly just approve and collect.
Everything here is copy-paste. I (the AI) draft and customize; you post and send.

## 1. The find-and-pitch method (ethical, repeatable)
Two paths — run both:

**Inbound (preferred — buyers come to you):** list productized gigs on Fiverr +
Etsy, link them in your clip-account bios. Reviews compound into steady inbound.
This is the closest thing to "say here's the product, get paid."

**Outbound (respond to public posts — never spam):** check public job boards and
reply to *posts that are actively asking* for the work.
- Upwork / Freelancer / PeoplePerHour job feeds (filter to your services).
- Reddit r/forhire and niche subs (reply to `[Hiring]` posts only).
- Local/industry Facebook groups where people ask for editors.
- Search queries to run weekly (paste into a search engine or the board):
  - `"need" (video editor OR clip) site:reddit.com/r/forhire`
  - `"looking for" product photo editing ecommerce`
  - `hiring short form video editor podcast`
> Rule: only reply where someone publicly asked. No cold-DMing strangers, no
> scraping behind logins — that gets accounts banned and breaks ToS.

## 2. Gig listing copy (Fiverr/Etsy) — template
```
Title:  I will [outcome] in [turnaround] — [niche]
        e.g. "I will turn your podcast into 5 viral captioned clips in 48 hours"
Tagline: Done-for-you [service]. Fixed price, fast turnaround, one revision.
What you get:
  • [deliverable 1]  • [deliverable 2]  • [deliverable 3]
Tiers:  Basic [scope/$] · Standard [scope/$] · Premium [scope/$]
Why me:  Fast, reliable, clear scope. [X]+ happy buyers.
To start: just send [the exact inputs needed] — see my intake questions.
```

## 3. Proposal template (for outbound replies)
```
Hi [name] — saw you need [task]. I do exactly this as a fixed-price service:
[deliverable] in [turnaround] for [$]. One revision included.
If helpful I can start as soon as you send [inputs]. Here's a recent example:
[portfolio link]. Want me to take it?
```
Keep it 4 lines. One clear offer, one clear ask, a proof link.

## 4. Intake form (kills back-and-forth)
Send immediately on order. Collect everything in one shot:
```
1. What exactly do you need delivered? (be specific)
2. Source files / links: [upload here]
3. Style/brand references or examples you like:
4. Format & specs: (dimensions, length, file type)
5. Deadline:
6. Anything to AVOID / not include:
7. Approved? Reply "GO" and I'll begin.
```

## 5. FAQ auto-replies (canned responses)
- **"How long?"** → "Turnaround is [X]. You'll get a preview before final."
- **"Revisions?"** → "One round included; extra rounds are [$] each."
- **"Can you do [out of scope]?"** → "Yes as an add-on for [$], or I can refer it."
- **"Discount?"** → "Prices are fixed for this scope, but the bulk/monthly tier
  is the better value if you need volume."
- **"Rush?"** → "24h rush is +[$]. Want me to add it?"

## 6. Delivery message template
```
Here's your finished [deliverable] ✅
[files/links]
Included: [recap of scope]. One revision is on the house — reply within [X] days.
If it's good to go, a quick review would mean a lot and helps me keep prices low.
Thanks for your order!
```

## 7. Payment protection (non-negotiable)
- On a marketplace: let **escrow** hold funds; deliver through the platform.
- Off-platform: **invoice upfront** (or 50% deposit) before starting. Stripe /
  PayPal invoice. Never deliver custom work to a stranger on a promise to pay.

## 8. What stays manual (be honest about this)
Final quality approval, judgment calls, anything needing your account/login, and
disputes. Automation handles the repetitive 80%; you handle the 20% that protects
your reputation and your money.

## Division of labor recap
- **I do:** draft listings, write proposals/replies, fill intake, produce the
  content/copy, draft delivery messages — customized each time you ask.
- **You do:** create the accounts, post listings, paste/send messages, run the
  editing apps for photo/video, approve, and collect payment.
